Parmesan Cheese Crisps Recipe


Ingredients:

  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder (optional for added flavor)
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano (optional for added flavor)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Prepare a Baking Sheet: Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or a silicone baking mat.
  3. Create Cheese Piles: Place small heaps (about 1 tablespoon each) of grated Parmesan cheese on the prepared baking sheet. Make sure to space them about 2 inches apart.
  4. Optional Seasoning: If desired, sprinkle a pinch of garlic powder and dried oregano over each cheese pile. This step adds extra flavor.
  5. Bake: Put the baking sheet in the preheated oven and bake for about 5-7 minutes or until the cheese piles melt and turn golden brown.
  6. Cool and Crisp: Remove the cheese crisps from the oven and let them cool for a minute or two on the baking sheet. As they cool, they will become crisp.
  7. Serve: Once they have cooled and crisped up, carefully lift the Parmesan cheese crisps from the baking sheet using a spatula.
  8. Enjoy: Enjoy your homemade Parmesan Cheese Crisps as a crunchy and savory snack!

These crisps are a delicious low-carb snack option and can be customized with various seasonings like paprika, rosemary, or red pepper flakes to suit your taste.
