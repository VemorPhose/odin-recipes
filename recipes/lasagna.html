Lasagna Recipe


Ingredients:

  • 1 pound (450g) ground beef or ground turkey
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 ounces) tomato paste
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 12 lasagna noodles, cooked and drained
  • 1 egg
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil or oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Make the Meat Sauce:\n
    • Cook the ground meat in a large pan until it's browned. Drain any excess fat.
    • Add chopped onions and cook until they're soft.
    • Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, dried basil or oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for about 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  2. Prepare the Cheese Filling:\n
    • In a bowl, mix together the ricotta cheese, egg, and half of the shredded mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses. Add a pinch of salt and pepper.
  3. Assemble the Lasagna:\n
    • Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
    • In a 9x13-inch baking dish, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce.
    • Spread half of the cheese mixture on top.
    • Add another layer of meat sauce.
    • Repeat with noodles, the remaining cheese mixture, and more meat sauce.
    • Finish with a layer of noodles and cover them with the rest of the meat sauce.
    • Sprinkle the remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses over the top.
  4. Bake:\n
    • Cover the dish with foil and bake for 25-30 minutes.
    • Remove the foil and bake for an extra 15-20 minutes until the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  5. Serve:
    • Let the lasagna cool for a few minutes before slicing.
    • Serve your simple and delicious homemade lasagna!

This recipe serves around 6-8 people, and it's perfect for a comforting family meal.
