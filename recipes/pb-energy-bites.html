Peanut Butter Energy Bites Recipe


Ingredients:

  • 1 cup old-fashioned rolled oats.
  • 1/2 cup creamy peanut butter (or any nut or seed butter of your choice).
  • 1/3 cup honey (or maple syrup for a vegan option).

Instructions:

  1. Mix Ingredients: In a mixing bowl, combine the rolled oats, peanut butter, and honey.
  2. Stir Well: Stir the ingredients together until they are evenly combined. The mixture should be thick and sticky.
  3. Form Bites: Take small portions of the mixture and roll them into bite-sized balls using your hands. You can make them as large or as small as you like.
  4. Chill: Place the energy bites on a tray or plate lined with parchment paper and put them in the refrigerator for about 30 minutes to firm up.
  5. Enjoy: Once chilled, your three-ingredient peanut butter energy bites are ready to enjoy! They make for a nutritious and satisfying snack.

Feel free to customize these energy bites by adding extras like chocolate chips, chopped nuts, dried fruits, or a sprinkle of cinnamon for added flavor and texture.
